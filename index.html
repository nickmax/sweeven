Our Story

Founded in 2023 in the heart of Tatu City, Sweeven Cafe blends modern Kenyan cuisine, specialty coffee, and chef-driven tasting menus. Our culinary team draws on international technique and locally sourced produce to deliver plates that delight every sense.

We believe in the power of food to bring people together, whether you are planning a coffee date, celebrating a milestone, or hosting a corporate dinner. Expect attentive service, curated wine pairings, and ambience that lingers long after dessert.
